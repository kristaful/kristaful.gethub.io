About Me

Hello! My name is Krista. I'm a self-motivated and personable full-time student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Missouri State University. Experienced in rising to the challenge in every position given to me, I left my mark with my previous company by redefining the scope of responsibilities for my role. Driven by my passion for efficiency without sacrificing quality, I find great fulfillment in high-performance atmospheres. Eager to establish long-lasting relationships, I would like to obtain a challenging but rewarding internship in cybersecurity or related field.


I'm the Vice President of the Association for Business Information Technology Students (A-BITS) while maintaining a 3.85 GPA. I am always seeking ways to further develop my skills in Information Technology. Indeed, I'm currently on a team preparing for the National Collegiate Cyber Defence Competition, taking place on February 19, 2022. Wish me good luck! My previous competition experiences include Hivestorm, an operating system hardening competition, and the U.S. Information Technology Collegiate Conference (USITCC), where I brought home the First Place award for PC Troubleshooting. We participated in numerous competitions over a period of three days in Amarillo, Texas. We competed against students from Texas State University, University of Houston, Oklahoma Panhandle State University, University of Arizona, and Amarillo College. The A-BITS students brought home 11 of the 22 awards, including overall best performing school. You can read more about this wonderful accomplishment on the College of Business News article written about the event.

I like to be creative in my personal time. My hobbies include reading and writing fantasy fiction, watching K dramas, or playing RPGs with my friends. I'm also a hobby photographer who has spent a lot of time photographing family and friends (several weddings), but what I like most is to capture the everyday life of people and things in a candid way.
