Hey there, my name is Krista.

You want to capture the moments that are the most important to you. You want the living memories of your life to stay still and be held.

I've had over a decade of experience in capturing the these moments in the most authentic way possible. I specialize in natural light, candid photography. I want you to be able to see and feel the magic in every photo, and keep these memories with you forever.
